About Us

Making great Kettle Cooked Potato Chips runs deep in our family. In 1970, Ted purchased his first potato chip company in Kenmore, OH, making multiple brands that were loved throughout the area. One of those brands was Hartville Potato Chips. Selling the business in 1991, Ted traveled the world helping others perfect the Kettle Cooked Potato Chip.

Now, with the help of his cousins, the family is back at it! We’re making a great tasting Kettle Cooked Potato Chip that will bring back memories of the way chips used to be made. After more than two decades of being away from the business, we hope you enjoy our new Hartville Potato Chip!

Ted Robb
1st Generation Chip Maker

Paul & Anthony LaGuardia

Location

1064 Grant St. Akron OH 44311

The South Akron neighborhood holds a bit of magic in our family. Two generations ago, Joe and Son’s Market was a staple corner market to neighbors along South Street. Stories of work in the store colored our youth. Yet, our family is not the only one to call the history of the Goose Town neighborhood a home. Klein’s Seafood and DiFeo & Sons Poultry continue to occupy the district. The tradition between the families embodies a warm embrace on holidays. ‘How’s business going?’ does not speak to the present, but conjures a thankfulness through the difficult seasons and a hope for the future.

In the city of comebacks, Akron sees the grandsons of Joseph and Rose LaGuardia return. This time cooking potato chips in a kettle, the determination of ancestry remains. The Grant Street neighborhood welcomes with open arms. With the support of neighbors, Klein’s and Hartville now share walls. Out both windows, one can view the DiFeo’s signs. All members are proud inhabitants of the South Akron District. Combining decades of tradition and new energy, we are proud to provide neighbors a local potato chip.
